Alien Invasion

  1. The aim of the game is to use the cannon to “shoot down” or destroy as many invading spaceships with correct answers, during a particular time frame. This must be done without any of the invading spaceships touching the cannon and/or the plank the cannon is on.
  2. You must use the left and the right arrow key to move the cannon. Once the cannon is moved or positioned underneath a spaceship then you must use the spacebar key to fire at the spaceship.
  3. Every time the cannon destroys a correct answer invading a spaceship, all the spaceship goes back to the top, new math problems are generated on all spaceships and a new answer is placed on the cannon.
